1️⃣ total = 0 kyu kiya?

Soch hume sum nikalna hai.

Maan le

low = 3
high = 8

Hume nikalna hai

3 + 4 + 5 + 6 + 7 + 8

Ab initially hamare paas koi sum hai hi nahi.

To hum ek variable banate hain

total = 0

Ye hamara sum store karne wala box hai.

Fir loop me

for i in range(low, high + 1):
    total += i

Har baar current number total me add hota jayega.

Iteration 1
i = 3

total = 0 + 3 = 3
Iteration 2
i = 4

total = 3 + 4 = 7
Iteration 3
i = 5

total = 7 + 5 = 12
Iteration 4
i = 6

total = 12 + 6 = 18
Iteration 5
i = 7

total = 18 + 7 = 25
Iteration 6
i = 8

total = 25 + 8 = 33

Finally

return total

Output

33

Isliye total = 0 se start kiya.

2️⃣ high + 1 kyu likha?

Ye Python ka sabse important concept hai.

Python me

range(start, stop)

stop value kabhi include nahi hoti.

Example

for i in range(1,5):
    print(i)

Output

1
2
3
4

Notice kiya?

5 print hi nahi hua.

Kyuki range() last value se pehle ruk jata hai.

Ab question me bola hai

3 se 8 tak sum nikalo.

Agar tum likhoge

range(3,8)

to output hoga

3
4
5
6
7

😅 8 to aaya hi nahi.

Isliye hum likhte hain

range(3,9)

ya

range(low, high+1)

Output

3
4
5
6
7
8

Ab 8 bhi include ho gaya.
